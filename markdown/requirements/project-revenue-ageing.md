### Business Requirements Document: Customer & Vendor Analysis

**1. Objective**
The objective of this project is to develop a dashboard to meet key customer requirements, including a dedicated report for customer ageing and a revenue analysis.

**2. Project Scope & Methodology**

* **Project Type:** This is a Proof of Concept (POC) project.
* **Data:** The dashboard will be designed and developed using only sample data.

**3. Key Deliverables & Requirements**

* **3.1 Revenue Analysis:** The dashboard must provide comprehensive analysis capabilities for revenue.
* **3.2 Customer Ageing Report:** The dashboard must include a dedicated report to analyze the ageing of customer accounts.
